Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a project take?

A landing page typically takes 2–5 days. A full web app depends on complexity — usually 1–3 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do you work remotely?

100%. I work with clients anywhere in Nigeria and beyond via email, WhatsApp, and video calls. Distance is never an issue.

What's your payment process?

Typically 50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. I accept bank transfer and mobile payment. Flexible for smaller projects.

Can you host/deploy the site too?

Yes! I can deploy to Netlify, Vercel, or GitHub Pages. For custom domains I'll guide you through the full setup.

What if I need changes after delivery?

Minor revisions are included for 7 days after delivery. Bigger changes are quoted separately — always fairly.

Do you do backend/full-stack?

My strength is frontend — HTML, CSS, JS, React. For backend needs I can connect you with trusted collaborators or advise on no-code solutions.
