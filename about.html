Hey, I'm Emmanuel 👋

I'm a frontend developer from Nigeria, known online and in creative circles as BlackSILV. I build web experiences that are fast, beautiful, and purposeful — the kind that people actually enjoy using.

I started coding out of curiosity and stayed because of the power it gives — the ability to take an idea from your head and put it in front of thousands of people with nothing but a browser. That still amazes me.

I'm currently a 100-level Electrical & Electronic Engineering student at the University of Uyo, which means I'm building at the intersection of software and hardware — and that gives me a different perspective from most frontend devs.

When I'm not coding, I'm sketching out technical drawings, learning something new, or thinking about the next wave to make. I believe technology should solve real problems, and I try to build with that in mind every single day.
